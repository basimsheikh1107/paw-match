Become a PawMatch Volunteer

Join Our Pack

At PawMatch, we're a community of passionate animal lovers dedicated to making a difference in the lives of pets in need. By joining our pack, you'll become part of a dynamic team of volunteers who share your passion for animal welfare. Together, we'll work towards creating a world where every pet has a loving home. As a volunteer, you'll have the opportunity to develop new skills, meet like-minded individuals, and experience the joy of knowing you're making a real difference. Whether you can spare a few hours or a few days, we invite you to join our pack and help us leave a paw-print of love and kindness.

Volunteer Opportunities

At PawMatch, we offer a range of volunteer opportunities to suit your skills, interests, and availability. Choose from the following roles:
1. Animal Care Assistant: Help care for our furry friends, including feeding, walking, and providing love and attention.
2. Adoption Counselor: Assist with matching pets with their forever homes, including conducting interviews and processing adoptions.
3. Event Volunteer: Participate in fundraising events, charity functions, and community outreach programs to help raise awareness and funds for our cause.
4. Administrative Assistant: Provide administrative support, including data entry, mailings, and other tasks to help keep our organization running smoothly.
5. Foster Care Provider: Open your home to provide temporary care and love for pets waiting for their forever homes.
6. Transportation Volunteer: Help transport pets to and from vet appointments, adoption events, and other destinations.

Get Started

Take the first step in joining our pack! Fill out our volunteer application form to share your story, passions, and interests with us, and discover how your unique talents can help us create a better world for animals.
